About Super Bob

Super Bob Inspections is dedicated to delivering thorough, honest, and timely inspections to help homebuyers make confident decisions. I inspect every property as if my own family were moving in, ensuring you have the peace of mind you deserve.

Our Services

Comprehensive Buyer Home Inspections

My detailed, top-to-bottom inspection provides a complete overview of your potential new home's condition. I examine everything from the foundation to the roof, giving you the crucial information and confidence needed to make a sound investment.

Walk and Talk Consultations

Perfect for either experienced or first-time buyers and investors who want to evaluate the house before committing. We'll walk through the property together, and I'll provide on-the-spot verbal assessments of major systems and visible concerns, empowering you to make fast, informed decisions.

Proactive Seller Pre-Listing Inspections

Identify and address potential issues before you list your home. A pre-listing inspection can help you streamline the selling process, justify your asking price, and avoid last-minute surprises or negotiations.

Radon Testing

Protect your family from this invisible health hazard. I offer professional radon testing using continuous monitoring equipment to accurately measure levels in the home, providing you with peace of mind and clear next steps if mitigation is needed.

Detached Garage & Outbuilding Inspections

Don't overlook the other structures on your property. I provide thorough inspections for detached garages, sheds, workshops, and other outbuildings to ensure they are safe, sound, and properly constructed.
